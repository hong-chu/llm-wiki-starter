## How it's organized
Five kinds of pages do most of the work:

- **Concepts** — ideas, frameworks, terminology. Stable; rarely deleted.
- **Entities** — people, orgs, products, papers, datasets. Factual;
  updated when new info arrives.
- **Syntheses** — thematic essays pulling from multiple sources.
  Opinionated; dated.
- **Comparisons** — side-by-side: A vs. B.
- **Questions** — open threads. Each has a current best answer and
  the evidence on either side.

Plus two utility pages — [[index]] for navigation and [[log]] for
the change history.
